Droids Robotics is a 5th year robotics team from Pittsburgh, PA, USA. We compete in FIRST LEGO League and participate in robotics-related events all year round. In FIRST LEGO League our team name is "Not the Droids You Are Looking For". We are honored to have won numerous robotics awards over the years. Most recently, we won the Champion's award (the highest award possible) at the international-level competition Razorback Open Invitational.

We are also the founders of EV3Lessons.com, a very popular robotics education website that has programming lessons available for the LEGO MINDSTORMS EV3. We have taught camps and workshops to students in Maryland, Virginia, Ohio and Pennsylania.
