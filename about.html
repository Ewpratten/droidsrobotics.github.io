Droids Robotics is a 5th year robotics team from Pittsburgh, PA, USA. They compete in FIRST LEGO League and are involved in robotics-related events all year round. They have represented the state of Pennsylvania and the United States at several international-level robotics tournaments hosted in Toronto (June 2014), Arkansas (May 2015) and St.Louis (April 2016).

Droids Robotics is also the founder of EV3Lessons.com, a very popular robotics education website that has over 50 programming lessons and dozens of resources for the LEGO MINDSTORMS EV3 available free of charge. The lessons are used by over 50,000 students in schools, robotics clubs, boy scouts merit badge workshops, and robotics teams. Droids Robotics and EV3Lessons have also provided programming workshops and demos for students in Pennsylvania, Maryland, Virginia and Ohio.

They really enjoy mentoring other teams, teaching students and being able to share their FIRST experience and knowledge with others.

For more information, visit www.droidsrobotics.org and ev3lessons.com. To contact them, email team@droidsrobotics.org

Award Descriptions:
Champion’s Award: "This award recognizes a team that embodies the FIRST LEGO League experience, by fully embracing our Core Values while achieving excellence and innovation in both the Robot Game and Project."

Robot Performance: "This award recognizes a team that scores the most points during the Robot Game. Teams have a chance to compete in at least three 2.5 minute matches and their highest score counts."

Innovative Solution: "This award recognizes a team’s solution that is exceptionally well-considered and creative, with good potential to solve the problem researched."
