Team History and Awards

Ten Years in FIRST

April 2019: Not the Droids You Are Looking For becomes a FIRST Robotics Competition Team
May 2018: Commendation from Governor of Pennsylvania
April 2018: Sixth Place Robot Performance, Champion's Award World Festival 2018
January 2018: First Place Robot Performance, First Place Champions
April 2017: Coach/Mentor Award World Festival St.Louis
June 2016: Global Innovation Award Top 20 Semi-Finalist, Washington, D.C.
April 2016: Sixth Place Robot Performance, First Place Programming World Festival St.Louis
January 2016: First Place Robot Performance, First Place Champion's
May 2015: Second Place Robot Performance, First Place Champion's Razorback Open Arkansas
January 2015: First Place Robot Performance, Second Place Champion's
June 2014: First Place Innovative Solution, International Open Toronto
February 2013: First Place Robot Performace, First Place Champion's
December 2012: Second Place Robot Performance, Second Place Champion's
December 2011: First Place Robot Performance, Third Place Champion's
August 2011: Not the Droids You Are Looking For is founded as a FIRST LEGO League team
