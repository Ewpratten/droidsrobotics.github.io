EV3Lessons.com

Our FLL World Class project was "How can we improve the way we learn to program the EV3?" We created over 40 beginner, intermediate, and advanced lessons. We also have many resources for teams including a Trash Trek scorer, Quick Guides, Core Values activities and more. It is currently used by over 30,000+ users from 124 countries and a go-to site for teams and students learning to program the EV3.
